Here are a few things I believe in:

  • 6 ingredients for success: Privilege, Hard work, Intelligence, Courage, Culture, Luck.
  • Seldom expect, always accept.
  • Follow your talent before you follow your passion.
  • 3 questions to check if everything is good:
    • When I go to bed, am I grateful for how the day went?
    • Am I sleeping well?
    • When I wake up, am I excited to work?
  • In this lifetime, this is the last time you will ever be as old as you are right now.
  • 3 Cs in visionaries: Convincing, Curious, Courageous.
  • Read more than you watch. Listen more than you speak.
  • Do at most 3 things at once: One which makes you happy, one which makes you rich, and one which makes you knowledgable. Ideally, you try to increase the overlap.
  • Be kind with those below you. Be confident with those above you.
  • Never drink alcohol. Never do drugs. Never kill something unless utmost necessary.
